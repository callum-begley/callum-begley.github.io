Blog 9: Foundations Reflection

Looking back over the last few weeks.

24 April 2025

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

I can do anything if I set my mind to it, I have been putting barriers on myself and that if I want to be a good programmer I will need to work well with other people.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values are important because they set the standard that you hold yourself to. Empathy is also important so you are aware of how others feel, and how the things you say and do effect them. This also ties in to self awareness.

What has surprised you the most about the Human Skills learning?

I have always thought my brain works a certain way and theres nothing I can do about it. But when learning about neuroplasticity, I realised I was just putting limitations on myself. I have always thought I was not creative even though I am into music, I would always put a barrier on myself and not even try creating music of my own.

What were the most challenging aspects of the Human Skills learning?

I don't usually write about myself and how I'm feeling so this was hard for me. I think that I am getting better at it with each reflection and new blog.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on Human Skills learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

Because programming is only half of it. Employers are looking for a well rounded individual who would be an asset to their team. They don't want to hire someone that is great at coding, but can't work in their team.

Does the time you spent studying Human Skills learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practice programming instead? Justify your answer.

No it isn't a waste of time. I will admit at the beginning I was a bit skeptical, but I do understand how important it is for career and self growth. If this course was just about coding, it wouldn't truly set us up for a successful coding career.
